The People Software Story

PEOPLE SOFTWARE (CIN: U61201PB2023PTC059699) was incorporated on October 3, 2023, with a singular vision: to eradicate network bottlenecks for Indian businesses and households.

Headquartered in Mohali, Punjab, we recognized that legacy ISPs were failing to provide the symmetrical speeds and low latency required by modern cloud applications. By laying our own high-capacity fiber backbone, People Software has rapidly grown into a preferred enterprise IT partner.
